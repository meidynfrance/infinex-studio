---

Two years ago, an AI writing assistant was a curiosity for early adopters. Today, it's as standard as a word processor. Teams that haven't integrated one are losing time to teams that have.

But the market is overcrowded. This guide focuses on the tools that genuinely matter for SMBs, organized by use case.

---

## Start With Your Primary Use Case

Before choosing a tool, identify what you're mainly writing:

- **Emails and client communication**: fast replies, follow-ups, outreach messages
- **Sales proposals and quotes**: structured, personalized content
- **Marketing content**: blog posts, social media, newsletters
- **Internal documents**: meeting notes, activity reports, summaries
- **Templated documents**: contracts, product sheets, presentations

Each category has its preferred tools.

---

## General-Purpose AI Assistants: ChatGPT and Claude

For most SMBs, a general-purpose assistant covers 80% of writing needs. ChatGPT (OpenAI) and Claude (Anthropic) are the two go-to references.

### Strengths for Professional Writing

**Claude** stands out on:
- Long-form, structured writing (proposals, reports, executive summaries)
- Consistent adherence to tone and formatting instructions
- Coherence across multi-page documents

**ChatGPT** is more versatile:
- Strong for short, punchy text (email subject lines, taglines, descriptions)
- Excellent for content ideation and brainstorming
- Better Microsoft ecosystem integration (Copilot)

**Pricing**: ~$20/month on personal plans, ~$25–30/user/month on team plans.

**Best for**: all SMBs. This is the universal starting point.

## Specialized Marketing Content Tools

If your priority is marketing content at volume — articles, web pages, social posts, newsletters — specialized tools add meaningful value.

### Jasper

One of the most mature tools on the market. Jasper offers **specialized templates** by content type (prospecting email, product description, SEO article, ad copy) and lets you define a "Brand Voice" for consistent tone across all generated text.

- **Strength**: ideal for marketing teams producing content at scale
- **Weakness**: high price (~$49/user/month minimum), poor fit for very small teams
- **Best for**: SMBs with a dedicated marketing team producing 20+ pieces of content per month

### Copy.ai

More accessible than Jasper, Copy.ai focuses on short-form text and sales outreach. Its **GTM AI Platform** helps structure outbound prospecting sequences.

- **Strength**: excellent for cold email and short sales copy
- **Weakness**: less suited to long, complex content
- **Pricing**: limited free plan, paid plans from ~$40/month

### Notion AI

If your team already uses Notion, the built-in AI integration is seamless. Write, summarize, rephrase, or translate directly in your workspace.

- **Strength**: zero friction — the AI is where you already work
- **Weakness**: less powerful than dedicated tools for highly specialized content
- **Pricing**: ~$10/user/month as an add-on to existing Notion plans

## Email and Outreach Specialists

### Superhuman with AI

Superhuman is a premium email client with built-in AI features: suggested replies, rephrasing, thread summaries. Its edge is speed — it's built for people whose primary work tool is their inbox.

- **Best for**: founders and salespeople processing 50+ emails per day
- **Pricing**: ~$30/user/month

### Lavender

A real-time coaching tool for outbound email writing. Lavender analyzes your emails as you type and suggests improvements to boost reply rates (length, structure, personalization).

- **Best for**: sales teams doing outbound prospecting
- **Pricing**: from ~$29/user/month

## Document Generation Tools

For SMBs that regularly produce sales proposals, quotes, or client presentations, dedicated tools go further than a general-purpose assistant.

### Gamma

Gamma generates **complete presentations** from a prompt or an outline. Layout is automatic, design is clean. In a few minutes, you have a client-ready deck.

- **Strength**: massive time savings on presentations
- **Pricing**: limited free plan, Pro at ~$10/month
- **Related**: see our article on [AI document generation](/blog/generation-documents-ia)

### PandaDoc with AI

PandaDoc is a sales document management platform (proposals, contracts, quotes). Its AI layer generates content sections from your templates and personalizes documents by prospect.

- **Best for**: sales teams sending more than 10 proposals per month
- **Pricing**: from ~$19/user/month

## How to Evaluate Writing Tool Quality

Perceived quality depends heavily on your criteria. Here are the four dimensions that matter for an SMB:

**1. Raw output quality**
Is the generated text usable without major rewriting? Test with a real use case: a client follow-up email, a section of your standard proposal.

**2. Tone and voice consistency**
Can the tool reproduce your style? Test by providing examples of your best existing writing as a reference.

**3. Workflow friction**
Does the tool integrate where you already work (email, CRM, office suite) — or does it require constant copy-pasting?

**4. Cost per content piece produced**
Calculate the time saved per generated piece. A $60/month tool that saves 2 hours per week is profitable for most SMBs.

## Which Writing Stack for Your Profile?

**Solo founder or salesperson**: Claude Pro or ChatGPT Plus. Simple, versatile, immediately useful.

**Small team (2–10 people)**: Claude Team or ChatGPT Team + Notion AI if you use Notion.

**Team with high marketing output needs**: Jasper or Copy.ai for volume, Claude for strategic content.

**Heavy outbound sales team**: Lavender for email coaching + a general-purpose assistant.
